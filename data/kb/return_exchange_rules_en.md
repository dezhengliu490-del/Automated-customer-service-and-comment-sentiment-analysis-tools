# Return & Exchange Rules Policy

## 1. General Provisions
- **7-Day No-Reason Return/Exchange:** Consumers may apply for a return or exchange without any reason within 7 days (inclusive) from the date of receipt, provided the product remains intact and does not affect secondary sales.
- **Quality Issue Return/Exchange:** If the product has quality issues that are not caused by human damage, consumers may apply for a return or exchange within 15 days from the receipt date. Beyond this period, it will be handled according to national warranty policies.

## 2. Conditions for Return & Exchange (Must all be met)
- The product must be unused, unwashed, unworn, and unassembled, with no obvious stains or artificial damages.
- The original packaging (e.g., shoeboxes, designated sealed bags, anti-counterfeit tags), hangtags, and manuals must be complete and not torn or altered.
- Any accompanying gifts, invoices, and vouchers must be returned together with the main product.

## 3. Items Exempt from 7-Day No-Reason Returns
According to relevant laws and industry standards, the following products do not support 7-day no-reason returns:
1. **Customized Products:** Items that have been specially manufactured, engraved, or altered in size according to the consumer's personal requirements.
2. **Perishable Goods:** Fresh and perishable items such as fruits, seafood, and live plants.
3. **Digital Goods:** Downloaded digital products or opened audiovisual media and computer software.
4. **Delivered Newspapers and Periodicals.**
5. **Items affecting secondary sales or personal safety when opened:** Such as opened intimate apparel (underwear, socks), cosmetics, food, and health supplements.

## 4. Shipping Fee Responsibilities
- **Non-Merchant Responsibility (Buyer's Personal Reason):** Returns or exchanges due to inappropriate size, personal dislike, or wrong selections. **The buyer is responsible for both the return shipping fee and the reshipment fee for exchanges.** If the item was originally free shipping, the buyer only pays for returning it; if it was not free shipping, the buyer bears the round-trip shipping fee.
- **Merchant Responsibility (Quality Issues/Wrong Shipments):** Returns or exchanges caused by product quality issues, or wrong/missing items sent by the merchant. **The merchant is fully responsible for round-trip shipping fees.**

## 5. Return & Exchange Process
1. **Initiate Request:** Buyers need to submit a "Refund/Return" application on the order page and select the corresponding reason. For quality issue returns, please upload clear photos or videos of the product as proof.
2. **Merchant Review:** The merchant's customer service will manually review the application within 24-48 hours.
3. **Return the Product:** Once approved, buyers should send the product back via courier within 7 days and accurately fill in the return tracking number in the system. (Note: **Do not use cash-on-delivery (COD) services, surface mail, or unapproved express services**, as the warehouse may reject the parcel.)
4. **Warehouse Receipt & Inspection:** Upon receiving the return package, the warehouse will conduct an unboxing quality inspection (usually takes 1-3 business days).
5. **Process Refund / Reshipment:**
   - **Refund Process:** After successful inspection, the system will refund the payment to the buyer's original payment account within 1-3 business days.
   - **Exchange Process:** After inspection, the warehouse will reship the requested size/color according to the buyer's system request, and notify the buyer of the new tracking number via SMS or message.

## 6. Special Precautions
- Please enclose a handwritten note inside the return package, clearly stating your **order number, contact phone number, and the reason for return/exchange (if exchanging, indicate the desired new color and size)**, so the warehouse can quickly locate your order and prioritize the process.
- Buyers should securely pack valuable or fragile items. If products are damaged during the return transit due to improper packaging, affecting secondary sales, the buyer must negotiate compensation directly with the logistics company.
