# Merchant Customer Service Scripts Knowledge Base

## 1. Greeting & Welcoming
- **Standard Greeting:** "Hi dear, welcome to our store! How can I help you today? Please feel free to let me know if you have any questions! ~"
- **Busy Response:** "Hi dear, we are currently experiencing a high volume of inquiries, our customer service team is replying as fast as possible. Please wait a moment, we will get back to you right away! [Heart]"
- **Returning Customer:** "Welcome back, dear! We have special exclusive discounts for our returning customers. What are you looking for today?"

## 2. Product Inquiries
- **Size/Material Recommendation:** "Hi dear! Could you please provide your height and weight, or your usual size? I can recommend the most suitable size and share details about the material for you."
- **Stock Confirmation:** "Hi dear, the item you are looking at is currently in stock! If you like it, you can place an order as soon as possible, and we will prioritize your shipment according to the order sequence."
- **Effects/Performance Inquiry:** "Hi dear, the main features of this product are its comfortable fit and soft material. Many of our regular customers have given great feedback. Of course, individual experiences may vary, you can refer to the buyer reviews on the product page!"

## 3. Order & Shipping Inquiries
- **Shipping Time:** "Hi dear, generally we will arrange shipping within 48 hours after your payment. If there are any special circumstances, we will notify you immediately. Please rest assured!"
- **Urging Shipment:** "Hi dear, I completely understand your urgency! I have already left an urgent note for our warehouse team to try and ship it out today. Please keep an eye on the logistics updates!"
- **Logistics Delays/Anomalies:** "Hi dear, I am so sorry for the inconvenience! I have contacted the logistics company to verify the situation. I will get back to you as soon as there is an update. Rest assured, we will take full responsibility!"

## 4. Negotiation & Discounts
- **Declining Bargains:** "Hi dear, I'm very sorry, but our store operates on low margins, and the current listed price is already our lowest promotional price. We cannot lower the price any further. We hope you understand! However, you can claim the store coupons first!"
- **Offering Small Gifts:** "Hi dear, we really can't go any lower on the price. How about this: if you place the order now, I will apply for a complimentary little gift to be sent along with your package. Would that work for you?"
- **Positive Feedback Request:** "Hi dear, if you have any questions after receiving the goods, please feel free to contact us! If you are satisfied with your purchase, we'd greatly appreciate your support by leaving a genuine review!"

## 5. After-sales & Complaints
- **Wrong/Missing Items:** "Hi dear, we are terribly sorry! Due to a high volume of shipments, our warehouse made a mistake, and we take full responsibility. Would you prefer a replacement, an exchange, or a refund for the price difference? Whatever is most convenient for you!"
- **Damaged/Quality Issues:** "Hi dear, we deeply apologize for the unpleasant shopping experience! Could you please send a clear photo of the damaged part? Once verified, we will definitely provide a satisfactory solution. We will cover the return shipping costs, so please don't worry!"
- **Soothing Emotions:** "Hi dear, I completely understand how you feel. I would be just as anxious if I were in your shoes. Please don't be angry, give us a chance to make it up to you. I will escalate and process this for you right away!"

## 6. Closing
- **Standard Closing:** "Thank you for your inquiry. If you have any other questions, feel free to contact me. Have a wonderful day!"
- **Nighttime Closing/Payment Reminder:** "Hi dear, it's getting late, sorry to disturb your rest. The item you are looking at has limited stock, so if you like it, you might want to place the order soon. Good night and sweet dreams!"
